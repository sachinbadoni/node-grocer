Accessibility Statement

**Date of Statement:** November, 2025

SmartGrocery.uk is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

1. Measures to Support Accessibility

We take the following steps to ensure accessibility of SmartGrocery.uk:

  • Incorporating accessibility into our development process.
  • Providing training to our staff on accessibility guidelines.
  • Conducting regular internal accessibility assessments.

2. Conformance Status

We are working towards conformance with the **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA**, an international standard for web accessibility.

3. Technical Specifications

Accessibility of SmartGrocery.uk relies on the following technologies:

  • HTML5 and CSS3
  • JavaScript (for interactive elements)

4. Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of SmartGrocery.uk. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers:

Email: support@smartgrocery.uk
