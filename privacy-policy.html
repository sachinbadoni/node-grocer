SmartGrocery.uk Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how SmartGrocery.uk (the "Platform," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our website and services, supporting local shops and connecting them with the community.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information You Provide Directly

  • **Contact Data:** When you sign up for early access, you provide your email address.
  • **Shop Data:** When shop owners register interest, they provide their shop name, type, and contact details.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our site, we may collect non-personal data like:

  • IP address and geographical location.
  • Browser type and operating system.
  • Pages viewed and time spent on the site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Facilitate the **early access program** and send you related offers.
  2. **Connect** interested shop owners with our onboarding team.
  3. **Improve** our website and services (using analytics data).

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • **Service Providers:** Third-party companies that perform services on our behalf (e.g., email hosting, analytics).
  • **Legal Compliance:** When required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your information.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@smartgrocery.uk
